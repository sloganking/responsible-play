# Gun Test

Self-check before play: **Would you trust yourself with a gun right now?** If not, should you be playing at MEDIUM [[severity-model|severity]] where unconscious mistakes have real consequences?

---

## The Logic

A gun operates at HIGH severity — one mistake is permanent. Intimacy operates at MEDIUM — one mistake has real impact. If you wouldn't trust yourself with a weapon, question whether you should be wielding intimacy.

Reasons you might fail the Gun Test:
- Sleeping meds or substances affecting awareness
- Emotional flooding / distress
- Exhaustion
- Hunger (physical or emotional)
- Activated trauma

Failing doesn't mean you can't participate. It means **choose consciously** — adjust what you do, tell your partner, take precautions. Don't sleepwalk.

---

## Gun Check (on Others)

You can also check whether someone else passes:
1. Acknowledge what you noticed
2. Inform your partner (if relevant)
3. Probe: "What's your plan if something goes sideways?"

Don't be a pest. But don't ignore what you see.
