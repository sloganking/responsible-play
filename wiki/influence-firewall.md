# Influence Firewall

Consent to ideas, not just touch. A tool for detecting whether someone's words install fear/urgency/guilt or observation/curiosity/verification.

---

## How It Works

Label what each sentence **installs** in your mind:

**Destructive pattern:** story → fear → urgency → guilt → recruitment
> "Jake was hanging around the new girl all night" → *installs suspicion*
> "I got a creepy vibe from him" → *installs fear*
> "Someone needs to say something before he does it again" → *installs urgency*
> "If you don't do anything, you're part of the problem" → *installs guilt*

**Constructive pattern:** observation → owned uncertainty → curiosity → verification → proportional step
> "I noticed Jake spent a lot of time with the new participant" → *observation*
> "Something felt off to me, but I'm not sure what" → *owned uncertainty*
> "Did anyone else notice? What did you see?" → *curiosity*
> "Should we check in with her and see how she experienced it?" → *verification*

---

## The Test: For Them or For Me?

Ask of any concerned message: **Is this oriented toward protecting someone, or toward recruiting me into a story?** The concerned path asks questions. The recruitment path installs conclusions.
