# RBDSMT

Pre-play conversation framework. Six topics to cover before intimacy. Based on ISTA practices.

---

## The Six Topics

| Letter | Topic | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **R** | Relationships | Who else is in your life? What agreements do you have? Hidden partners = hidden risk |
| **B** | Boundaries | What's off-limits? Hard boundaries don't get undone mid-scene (subtract, not add) |
| **D** | Desires | What do you want? What would make this a [[rbdsmt#good-thing\|good thing]]? |
| **S** | Sexual Health | STI status, testing, safer sex practices |
| **M** | Meaning & Mistakes | What does this experience mean to you? How do you handle it if something goes wrong? |
| **T** | Trauma | Anything I should know about your history that might surface? Known triggers? |

---

## Good Thing

Defined as: an experience where everyone feels good **during AND after**. The standard for play. If it only felt good during but terrible after — it wasn't a good thing.

---

## Couples as Double Risk

Playing with someone in a couple means you're also playing with their partner's [[filters-and-ras|filters]], jealousy, and [[complementary-filters|patterns]]. A couple that passed RBDSMT individually can still blow up if the partner's stories activate after the fact.
