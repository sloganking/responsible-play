# Logan King

Author of *The Wolf Who Cried Boy*. Self-described "Seer" and "belief doctor" — sees the invisible beliefs running people's lives and shows them how to cut the strings.

---

## Personal Story

First 25 years: victim mentality, panic attacks (8 hours, every other day), 7-year dry spell, beliefs of powerlessness and unlovability. PTSD, borderline suicidal.

**The journey:** 5 hours/week therapy for a year (helped intellectually, didn't change body patterns). Landmark Forum. Tony Robbins. Then: play parties as self-directed exposure therapy. Each experience built evidence against the old beliefs.

**The crystallizing moment:** At a retreat, witnessed someone through distorted beliefs think they were protecting someone while deeply harming someone else. His father and him, replayed in a different context.

**What he learned:** Most harm comes from love. His father was a [[righteous-predator|Rescuer]] — scared parent, loving intentions, violent methods, devastating outcomes.
