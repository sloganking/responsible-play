# 100% Control

Both parties can independently stop or create an outcome. Not zero-sum — both hold 100% simultaneously.

---

## The Dance Metaphor

A dance requires both people. Either one saying no stops the dance. That means each person independently has 100% control over whether the dance happens. Both 100%'s exist at the same time without conflicting.

**Prevention side:** You can always stop an outcome you don't want. You have [[fawning|100% control]] to say no, to leave, to set a boundary.

**Creation side:** You can't force a specific partner to dance with you. But you can keep asking until someone says yes. You have 100% control over whether you get the outcome eventually — just not with whom or when.

---

## The Power of Seeing Your 100%

When you believe "they had 100% control so I had 0%" — you've given away your power. Seeing your 100% ends the victim freeze and enables creation and prevention. The victim feeling often tracks unused power, not absent power.

This is NOT about absolving others of their responsibility. Their 100% exists too. It's about seeing YOUR power so you can use it.
